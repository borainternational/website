OUR VISION

Disrupted minds; High performing youth; Transformed societies

OUR MISSION

Leveraging transformative knowledge, skills and connections for youth advancement. We aim to offer support to Africa’s youth that ignites growth within themselves and their communities

YOUTH DEVELOPMENT

We support positive youth advancement by providing them with transformative knowledge, information, skills and connections they need to reach their potential. We work with local and international groups to administer programs and offer support and service that increase young people’s abilities to prepare for their future, obtain meaningful work, engage and be competitive in value chains and become leaders in their communities.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

We believe women are key contributors to improving household livelihoods and now industry competitiveness. Active economic participation of this subordinate group ... enables societies to grow and achieve positive results across economic and social indicators. Our “She” and inclusion initiatives, support women in the workforce while a variety other programs offers our services to girls and women at little or no cost. We also believe that the extreme challenges that the girl-child faces today is what makes them a subordinate group and through shining a new ray of hope for the future, working through a plan, building their skills and self-confidence, we empower them to make important decisions about their economic and social wellbeing.

ENTERPRENEURSHIP AND ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT

We equip aspiring and practicing youth entrepreneurs with tools to identify, jumpstart and take advantage of viable business opportunities. ... Through offering technical assistance we help them increase their competitiveness and successfully navigate the market.

WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT

We build capacity for local and multinational companies through leadership and new-entrants trainings for youth in the workforce. Our focus on transition trainings equip youth with soft but necessary skills to navigate and succeed in new workspaces and/or positions of leadership. By doing this, we bridge the skills gap in the workplace, ensuring that the workforce respond to the demands of the corporate market.
